No Man's Sky is a game about exploration and survival, set in an infinite universe. You can fly seamlessly from the surface of a planet to another, and every star in the sky is a sun that you can visit.

Where you'll go and how fast you'll make your way through this universe is up to you. It's yours for the taking.
